Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our catering services and packages

Our minimum catering requirement is 25 guests for most packages. For smaller intimate gatherings, we offer special arrangements with a minimum order value. Corporate lunch boxes can be arranged for as few as 10 people.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for regular events. For wedding catering, please book 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability and proper planning. Rush orders may be accommodated based on availability with an additional fee.

Yes! While we specialize in non-vegetarian Madurai cuisine, all our packages include traditional vegetarian dishes. We can also create completely vegetarian menus and ensure separate preparation areas for religious functions when required.

We provide catering services throughout Madurai city and surrounding areas up to 50km. For events beyond this range, additional transportation charges may apply. We also cater to destination events with advance planning.

Absolutely! Please inform us about any food allergies or dietary restrictions when booking. We can modify our recipes and ensure separate preparation for allergen-free dishes. Common accommodations include dairy-free, nut-free, and gluten-sensitive options.

Our service staff includes trained servers, kitchen assistants for food warming and presentation, and cleanup crew. For live counters, we provide specialized chefs. Staff-to-guest ratios vary by package: 1:25 for basic service, 1:15 for premium service.
